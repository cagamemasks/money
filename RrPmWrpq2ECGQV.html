TACTICS OF BOLLINGER BANDS

In Forex trading, there are many trading indicators that are trusted by traders. The tactics of Bollinger bands belong to this category - powerful analytical tools that can significantly simplify forecasting and reduce risks in the process conclusion of trade transactions. In fact, Bollinger bands are based on the action of two forces that have a significant impact on chart changes - indicators of the main axis of the price range of the current trend and changes in indicators (compression/expansion) during the movement of price values. It is based on the interaction of these factors these two factors create patterns on the charts that line up when bars pass through the set boundaries.

The Bollinger bands are three lines designed for the most accurate determination of extremes price values for the current time period and are the most effective short-term indicators within the framework of technical Analytics tools. Accordingly, the upper and lower bands determine the oversold/overbought boundaries of the traded asset and the price movement within them can be fairly reliable indicate the current state of the market situation at a specific time.Â 

The tactics of Bollinger bands allow us to effectively use them in active interaction with other indicators, in particular, with Japanese candlesticks, which allow us to predict market changes in the near future. The effectiveness of Bollinger bands as indicators is due to the speed of their reaction to changes in price indicators. The wave-like curves of the figures of this indicator allow you to predict the range of movements of the trend with a high degree of probability, but only if the trader has sufficient experience of interpretation values of such graphical constructions.

Experience with Bollinger bands is especially important when the price band is compressed . in this case, you can expect a stop in the trend movement, while the strength of the impact of band compression on the price can be different and calculate exactly how large the impact will be. delicate. But the effort to master the tactics of Bollinger bands is worth it - it is this indicator that makes it possible to determine the presence of implicit price fluctuations as accurately as possible and allows you to establish a positive trend towards profit within the framework of making a deal.

But it is worth remembering about this feature of the application tactics of Bollinger bands, such as higher accuracy in determining time indicators, faster accuracy in determining price parameters. Fluctuations in the bands can be limited by a small distance from the local maximum or minimum values, and even produce a pullback of positions, which presages the proximity of a new one the momentum of the current trend. At the same time, price values are very often not able to reach a new level of minimum or maximum until there is a graphical expansion of the bands, which means that there are opportunities for free movement of the price.

When choosing the tactics of Bollinger bands, it is very important for a trader to track chart changes. In particular, there are significant differences in aspiration the upper borders of the bands and other indicators usually serve as a signal for a possible breakout, the moment of which, as a rule, falls on the price reaching the level of the band's base. It is also important to track the level of slope of the bands when bars reach / approach the levels of reaching the price minimum/maximum. The most effective use of the Bollinger bands tactic is achieved by tracking the change indicators real-time charts. The Bollinger bands are also useful in early forecasting of a trend change. A noticeable change in the the speed of movement of price indicators is recorded on the chart by a significant increase in the width of the bands, followed by their narrowing when market activity changes. It is the combination of these factors that can serve as a preemptive signal for the trader, allowing him to determine the moment of trend change with a fairly high accuracy, marked by rapid and short market movement in vertical dynamics.
