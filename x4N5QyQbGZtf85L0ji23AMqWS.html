TOP 8 free resources for trading on the Russian market

TOP 8 free resources for trading on the Russian market

I often get a question from clients: which sites are better to use for analyzing the Russian market. In this article, I will describe several sites that can be used to analyze the Russian market shares, bonds and derivatives markets. I will note that this article will not consider paid sites, but will describe only free ones.

We will move in practice from General to specific.

Website Of The Central Bank Of The Russian Federation.

If You are in your own if you use macroeconomics for trading, then you need a Central Bank website. The site contains a lot of information about the economic situation in Russia, the development of the financial market, and information about plans to issue Federal loan bonds. The regulator's portal reflects important indicators: inflation, prices for precious metals, RUONIA and much more. Here you can also find information on purchasing gold and currency.

Website Of The Ministry Of Finance Of The Russian Federation.

If You are a conservative investor and your portfolio of bonds Federal loan on this website You will find all information about upcoming issues of securities. Also on this site is the history of auctions of bonds already traded on the exchange. Plus, the Ministry of Finance website contains a lot of statistical information that helps with macroeconomic analysis.

Official website of the Moscow exchange.

This site needs use it mainly to track news about changes in regulations on the Moscow exchange or to Refine the specification of contracts.

For example, let's take a recent case on the urgent section. Before the new year, on December 25, the Moscow exchange increased the guarantee coverage for oil futures, few of them market participants tracked this event, did not follow the news on the website of the Moscow exchange as a result, a total margin call began on the market.

In practice, I use this site to track the trading volume and schedule of the exchange. Earlier on the website of the stock exchange was presented with the prospectuses of issuing securities however, these prospectuses have recently been removed and can no longer be found on the website.

sMart-lab.ru

This site can be viewed from different angles. By how much it can be used, both for communicating with other traders, in particular with highly speculative people, and for investing and analyzing securities debt and equity markets.

I often use tables on this site with Federal loan bonds, sub-Federal bonds, and corporate securities. Data the table shows the issues in a very convenient form and gives fairly detailed information about each of the papers.

Finam.

To unfortunately, not all information is reflected on the Smart lab, so you have to search for data on third-party sites. On Finam's website, you can find a lot of useful information about bonds and shares.

This website can be used to find information about the offer for bonds and download their prospectus emissions. But the portal contains all the information about the economy of Russia and the world.

BCS Express.

Do you need to find high-quality Analytics for all Russian and foreign markets? Then you will definitely go to this site. This site provides full functionality, even without opening a brokerage account with BCS, you need to just go through the usual registration.

Many of my friends at BCS track the dividend calendar, and also use the signals of analysts of this portal. This site can be used in the web version or via the my broker app.

Investfund

The site provides a lot of features, but I use it for for a single purpose: viewing the company's corporate reports listed on the Moscow exchange stock exchange section. Without registration, you can view the Issuer's financial statements for 5 years here. In fact, it is very helpful for fundamental analysis of securities.

Investing.

For many traders, this the site is the main working tool. To be honest, I myself have a portfolio of Russian stocks to watch. Investing is great for working with technical analysis.

All the sites mentioned above we use for the analysis of the Russian securities market, all sites are free of charge, if You want to dive into the analysis of securities is much deeper, so I recommend opening the official websites of issuers. They contain much more quality information and less empty Analytics.
